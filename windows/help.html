HSMSE Homework Checker

A tool to help track assignments across HSMSE accounts

⚠️ Important Notice
This is NOT a professional app - it's just a tool that one parent is sharing to hopefully help others out. This tool is provided as-is with no guarantees. Use at your own risk!

What It Does

This app simulates logging in and looking for all missing and upcoming assignments across various HSMSE accounts (Google Classroom, Jupiter Ed, etc.).

Important Limitations

  • This is an inherently brittle process that WILL break for all sorts of reasons
  • It can and probably will miss some assignments and get details wrong
  • If any of the sites are down, updates will fail
  • If a teacher changes where they are posting assignments, they may not be found
  • The app is UNSIGNED and your operating system will make you jump through security hoops to install it
  • I can't help if you run into problems!

First Time Setup

  1. Start the updating assignments process by clicking the "Update Assignments Now" button.
  2. You will be asked for your Jupiter credentials and to choose which classes to load from Jupiter Ed. Choose the classes that are primarily updated on Jupiter -- if you include classes that are primarily updated on Google Classroom, you may end up with duplicate assignments.
  3. You will be asked to log in to an @nycstudents.net account for the NYC Students Google Classroom
  4. You will then be asked to add on your @hsmse.org account for the HSMSE Google Classroom
  5. NOTE: all these credentials are stored locally on your own computer and are not shared with anyone. I have no access to them.

Once this is properly configured, you'll only have to log in again when the cookies expire.
